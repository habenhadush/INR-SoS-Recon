K-Wave Generalization Gap — Reference Papers
==============================================
Collected 2026-02-28 during IC-to-kwave failure analysis

1. Byra et al. (2024) — INR for SoS Estimation
   "Implicit Neural Representations for Speed-of-Sound Estimation in Ultrasound"
   Key: Per-case INR with differentiable beamformer; avoids fixed L-matrix
   https://arxiv.org/abs/2409.14035

2. Learned Correction Methods for USCT (Feb 2025)
   "Learned Correction Methods for Ultrasound Computed Tomography Imaging Using Simplified Physics Models"
   Key: Data correction (preprocess d_meas) generalizes better than artifact correction (post-hoc CNN)
   https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.09546

3. P2INR-FWI — MICCAI 2025
   "P2INR-FWI: an Implicit Neural Representation for Full-Waveform Inversion in USCT"
   Key: Polar-coordinate INR + structural prior; mitigates cycle-skipping
   https://papers.miccai.org/miccai-2025/paper/2163_paper.pdf

4. Plug-and-Play Untrained NN for FWI (2024)
   "A Plug-and-Play Untrained Neural Network for Full Waveform Inversion in Reconstructing Sound Speed Images of Ultrasound Computed Tomography"
   Key: Network architecture as implicit regularizer (Deep Image Prior); early stopping critical
   https://arxiv.org/abs/2406.08523

5. Physics and Deep Learning in Computational Wave Imaging (2024)
   Key: Survey of physics-based vs deep-learning approaches for wave imaging
   https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.08329

6. Robust DL for Pulse-echo SoS via Time-shift Maps (2024)
   Key: Time-shift DL resilient to resolution mismatch, generalizes across forward model variations
   https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12629671/

7. 3-D Forward Model with Transducer Modeling (IEEE TUFFC 2023)
   "A Forward Model Incorporating Elevation-Focused Transducer Properties for 3-D FWI in USCT"
   Key: 2D/3D model mismatch causes significant artifacts; 3D forward model needed
   https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10775680/

8. DNN-based SoS with Tikhonov Pseudo-Inverse Prior (2023)
   "Deep-Learning-Based Ultrasound Sound-Speed Tomography Reconstruction with Tikhonov Pseudo-Inverse Priori"
   Key: Combining classical regularization with DL reconstruction
   https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10448397/


Phase 2: Displacement Field Denoising — Reference Papers
=========================================================
Collected 2026-03-01 during measurement-domain correction research

9. Ring Artifact Removal via INR Sinogram Decomposition (2024)
   "Ring Artifacts Removal Based on Implicit Neural Representation of Sinogram Data"
   Key: Two INRs decompose sinogram into ideal signal + stripe artifacts;
        directional regularization (smooth signal, sparse artifacts); unsupervised
   https://arxiv.org/abs/2409.15731

10. Diff-INR: Generative Regularization for EIT (2024)
    "Diff-INR: Generative Regularization for Electrical Impedance Tomography"
    Key: INR + diffusion prior for inverse problem; bandwidth control via positional encoding;
         robust to noise (40-60 dB SNR); mesh-resolution agnostic
    https://arxiv.org/abs/2409.04494

11. Untrained NN for Inverse Problems with Model Mismatch (2024)
    "Solving Inverse Problems with Model Mismatch using Untrained Neural Networks within Model-based Architectures"
    Key: Untrained forward model residual block corrects model mismatch per-instance;
         joint optimization of correction + reconstruction; 2-3 dB PSNR improvement
    https://arxiv.org/abs/2403.04847

12. FINER: Spectral-Bias Tuning in INR (CVPR 2024)
    "FINER: Flexible spectral-bias tuning in Implicit NEural Representation by Variable-periodic Activation Functions"
    Key: INRs inherently fit low-frequency first (spectral bias); can be exploited for denoising;
         variable-periodic activations allow frequency control
    https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2024/papers/Liu_FINER_Flexible_Spectral-bias_Tuning_in_Implicit_NEural_Representation_by_Variable-periodic_CVPR_2024_paper.pdf

13. Deep Image Prior Survey (2025)
    "A Survey on Deep Image Prior for Image Denoising"
    Key: Untrained networks leverage architectural bias as implicit prior;
         zero-sample learning; spectral bias = natural denoiser with early stopping
    https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S105120042500257X

14. NN-Based Regularization for Inverse Problems in Imaging (2024)
    "Neural-network-based regularization methods for inverse problems in imaging"
    Key: Survey of learned vs untrained NN regularizers; data correction in measurement domain
         preferred over post-hoc image correction
    https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.1002/gamm.202470004

15. Accelerated INR for CT Reconstruction (2025)
    "Accelerated optimization of implicit neural representations for CT reconstruction"
    Key: INR implicit regularization prevents overfitting to noise/artifacts in reconstruction
    https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.13390
